Who We Are

A Builder with a Developer’s Perspective

LV Construction is a subsidiary of Six Peak Capital, a Los Angeles–based real estate investment and development firm.

Founded in 2018, LV Construction is a ground-up general contractor serving Southern California. Our team brings decades of experience building across the region—from wood-frame and podium structures to concrete construction.

Because our parent company is an active developer, we understand the nuances of how development partners need to capitalize and build their projects. We know what it takes to raise equity and secure debt, and we structure our approach to strengthen a developer’s position throughout the process—from permitting through groundbreaking and beyond.

Our affordable housing platform operates as LV Affordable Construction LP, with dedicated LIHTC experience and the specialized compliance knowledge that tax credit projects demand.

Our Approach

Partnership, Not Competition

We don’t compete against other general contractors for your project. We partner with vetted development teams and build together.

Traditional procurement pits GCs against each other in a competitive bid—often at the expense of transparency and long-term alignment. We take a different approach. LV Construction works exclusively in a partnership format with development partners we know and trust. The competitive bidding happens where it matters most: at the subcontractor level, where our deep trade relationships produce genuinely competitive pricing through open-book transparency.

This model creates the pricing environment developers seek while preserving the alignment that makes projects run smoothly. When a developer and GC share full visibility into how a project is actually bid, priced, and built, the result is a stronger capital stack, cleaner draws, and a project that lenders and equity partners can underwrite with confidence.

1

Partnership Alignment

Project goals, engage as a partner, not a bidder

We begin with an honest conversation about your project goals, capital structure, and timeline. If there is a fit, we engage as your construction partner, not a competing bidder.

2

Preconstruction and Budgeting

Proprietary cost data estimates to position your project for equity and debt at no cost

We deliver a detailed conceptual estimate by CSI division using our proprietary existing project cost data, allowing the developer partner to finalize achievable underwriting to help position the project for equity and debt conversations at no cost. Full preconstruction services are available for projects that have already achieved commitments for project capitalization.

3

Project Capitalization

Six Peak helps advise on capitalization strategies

In partnership with Six Peak, we help advise on capitalization strategies, and in select circumstances provide credit enhancement and capital for projects. A successful commitment for equity and debt capitalization trigger the competitive subcontractor bidding process, lining it up to coincide with construction loan closing.

4

Competitive Subcontractor Bidding

Scoped bids to vetted trade base with full transparency and open book pricing

We issue scoped bid packages to our vetted trade base, receive competitive proposals, and level bids scope for scope. Every number is transparent, you see exactly what we see. We can provide advised detail about which subs are best for each project subject to cost and availability. Full bidding takes 7 to 9 weeks and is most productive as the project approaches RTI and has fire department approval.

5

GMP and Capital Support

Guaranteed maximum price delivered and structured for lender confidence

We present a Guaranteed Maximum Price with open book accounting and a complete project schedule, structured to give your lenders and equity partners the confidence they need to fund timely construction draws once the project commences.

6

Mobilization

Contracts finalized, buyouts commence, site groundbreaking

We finalize subcontractor contracts and begin buyout, followed by mobilization of the site shortly after financing closes.

7

Construction and Closeout

Full project management to CO, real time tracking and schedule transparency

Full project management through certificate of occupancy. Weekly OAC meetings, real time budget tracking, and proactive schedule management, with no surprises.

Our Vetting Process

  • Financial qualification — We verify bonding capacity, insurance coverage, and financial stability before any subcontractor is invited to bid.
  • Track record review — We evaluate past project performance, references, and on-time completion history across similar project types.
  • Safety record — EMR ratings, OSHA compliance history, and site safety programs are reviewed for every trade partner.
  • Long-term relationships — Over 70% of our subcontractors have worked with us on 3+ projects, ensuring consistency and reliability.
  • Competitive pricing — Repeat relationships give us preferred pricing without sacrificing quality. Our subs know our standards.
